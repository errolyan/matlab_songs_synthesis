Group delay parameters (*)

One unique (at least in 1996) feature of STRAIGHT is the group delay manipulated excitation source signal. The signal is controlled by the three parameters, groupDelayStandardDeviation, groupDelayRandomizeCornerFrequency and groupDelaySpatialBandWidth. The following three subsections illustrates effect of controlling each parameter.

Please keep the flag variable ratioModeIndicator unchanged, because the other group delay parameter ratioToFundamentalPeriod was found to be problematic and is usually not in use.


Subsections
  • Parameter: groupDelayStandardDeviation (*)
    • groupDelayStandardDeviation=0.5 ms (default )
    • groupDelayStandardDeviation=2 ms
    • groupDelayStandardDeviation=6 ms

  • Parameter: groupDelayRandomizeCornerFrequency (*)
  • Parameter: groupDelaySpatialBandWidth (*)
    • groupDelaySpatialBandWidth = 7Hz
